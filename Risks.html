Kloud Risk Management

Kloud Komputing adheres to every state and country's regulations and security measures. As technology continues to advance, so do bad actors; however, Kloud Komputing places an increased focus on 5-Key areas to protect your organization:

  1. Technology
  2. Operational
  3. Financial
  4. Data Security and Regulatory
  5. Vendor

For more information on costs.


Technology

The Technology risks associated with the cloud are around the evolving of technology asscoiated with cloud infrastructure and security. Kloud will provide standardization around upgrading and updating technology associated with your Kloud solutions.

Operational

The Operational risks are associated with the execution of IT services and tasks organization's rely upon. The Kloud solutions are tightly integrated with the most up-to-date DevOps offerings, which will provide your organization with faster deploy times.

Financial

The Financial risks associated with the Kloud revolve around costs of utilization. The Kloud solution monitors cloud usage and reports on usage and ensures unused resources are minimalized.

Data Security and Regulatory

The Data Security and Regulatory risks include data loss, leakage, or unavailability of data. These issues can have severe consequences to organizations and customers. Kloud provides solutions that adhere to all state and federal regulations.

Vendor

The Vendor risks associated with the cloud include working with and doing business with vendors, which open up additional risks to organizations. Kloud will provide an extra layer of protection to ensure the right accesses and permissions are in place.
